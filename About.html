ABOUT US

Hey guys you are warmly welcome to AccomadateMe. Here we are trying to help to find the best boding place to you for your undergraduate life. Our web site provides a easier way to find boding places by yourself.

Here we introduce you a facility of creating accounts and maintain them by your own.You can create a new Account by going to Sign Up page. ALso you can log in to them by using Log In page.We are provides the best security for your AccomadateMe account. This web site is opened for boding owners as well as Undergraduates of University of Moratuwa.

Boding owners can create an account called Boding owner account and add their boding places with details and images and videos to their own page. Comments and reviews are also can be posted in comments section. There is a feature to filter valid comments and put them into the comments forum. No space to invalid comments.

Students can also create their accounts as student accounts.They can find a proper boding place with their requirements by filling requierment section in the forum. They can also put their comments and reviews in comment section. Things regarding comments are valid for both boding owners and students. Be kind enough to put valid comments.

You can easily deactivate your account using deactivate feature.
